This archive of previously issued food recalls and allergy alerts is provided for reference and research purposes.
Users should note that the products listed in the archive have been subject to removal from the marketplace or appropriate corrective action. Food recalls or allergy alerts are not an indication of the food safety status of products produced at a later date.
OTTAWA, October 10, 2001 - The Canadian Food Inspection Agency (CFIA) is warning consumers not to consume certain brands of Olive-Pomace Oil due to the presence of polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons (PAHs), some of which are carcinogenic.
The affected products are known to have been distributed in Ontario and Quebec and may have been distributed nationally. The following Olive-Pomace Oil products are affected by this alert:
BRAND
PRODUCT NAME
COUNTRY OF ORIGIN
SIZE
CODES
Olive-Pomace Oil, is a lower grade oil. It is obtained from the residue (pomace) left after the extraction of higher grades of olive oil such as Virgin Olive Oil. Higher grade oils sold as Olive Oil, Virgin Olive Oil and Extra Virgin Olive Oil are not affected by this alert.
PAHs are a group of chemicals that are formed during certain industrial processes and are common environmental contaminants. Some of the PAHs are carcinogenic and long term exposures to PAHs can lead to serious health risks.
The CFIA is currently taking measures to have the affected products recalled from the marketplace. The CFIA will be monitoring the effectiveness of the recalls.
For more information, consumers and industry can call the CFIA at one of the following numbers:
In the province of Quebec 1-800-561-3350; or In other provinces and territories 1-800-442-2342.
For information on receiving recalls by electronic mail, or for other food safety facts, visit our web site at www.inspection.gc.ca.

	1.	Please assign labels to the columns "productCategory" and
    		"hazardCategory" describing the contents of the text.
    		Values for the field "productCategory" should be chosen from the following:
    
		 - prepared dishes and snacks
		 - meat and meat products (other than poultry)
		 - non-alcoholic beverages
		 - cocoa and cocoa preparations, coffee and tea
		 - confectionery
		 - cereals and bakery products
		 - soups, broths, sauces and condiments
		 - fish and fish products
		 - herbs and spices
		 - poultry meat and poultry meat products
		 - crustaceans and products thereof
		 - honey and royal jelly
		 - other food product / mixed
		 - fats and oils
		 - dietetic foods, food supplements, fortified foods
		 - bivalve molluscs and products therefor
		 - fruits and vegetables
		 - ices and desserts
		 - pet feed
		 - milk and milk products
		 - nuts, nut products and seeds
		 - sugars and syrups
		 - food contact materials
		 - alcoholic beverages
		 - soups
		 - food additives and flavourings
		 - eggs and egg products
		 - feed materials
		 - cephalopods and products thereof
		 - feed additives

    		Values for the field "hazardCategory" should be chosen from the following:
    
		 - biological
		 - allergens
		 - fraud
		 - chemical
		 - foreign bodies
		 - organoleptic aspects
		 - food contact materials
		 - other hazard
		 - migration
		 - food additives and flavourings
		 - packaging defect

    	2.	Please copy and paste the text span(s) from the text that
    		are most descriptive of the respective assigned label into the columns
    		"productSpan" and "hazardSpan".Spans should be separated by linefeeds.
